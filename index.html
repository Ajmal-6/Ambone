🩹 Be Prepared
FIRST AID
QUICK GUIDE

While waiting for the ambulance, these actions can save a life. Share with everyone.

❤️

CPR — Cardiac Arrest

30 chest compressions, 2 rescue breaths. Push hard and fast at center of chest. Continue until help arrives.

🧠

Stroke — FAST Test

Face drooping · Arm weakness · Speech slurring · Time to call 108 immediately.

🩸

Severe Bleeding

Apply firm, direct pressure with clean cloth. Do not remove the cloth. Elevate the limb above heart level.

🔥

Burns

Cool with running water for 20 minutes. Do NOT use ice, butter, or toothpaste. Cover loosely with cling film.

❓ FAQ
COMMON
QUESTIONS
How fast will an ambulance arrive? +
Our average response time in major cities is 6–10 minutes. Response time depends on your location, traffic, and nearest unit availability. We continuously optimize routing using AI dispatch.
Can I track the ambulance in real time? +
Yes! After booking, you receive an SMS with a live tracking link. You can share this with family members. The link updates the ambulance position every 5 seconds.
What payment methods are accepted? +
We accept cash, credit/debit cards, UPI, net banking, and most major insurance providers. You can also use your AmbuOne membership for zero-copay trips.
Do you provide inter-city transport? +
Yes. We offer inter-city and inter-state transport for both emergency and non-emergency cases. Air ambulance services are available for urgent long-distance transport.
Are your ambulances equipped with advanced gear? +
All units are equipped per national standards. ALS units carry cardiac monitors, defibrillators, ventilators, IV medications, and oxygen. Equipment is checked and restocked after every run.
How do I cancel or reschedule a booking? +
For scheduled bookings, you can cancel or reschedule via SMS reply, WhatsApp, or by calling our dispatch center. Emergency bookings cannot be cancelled once an ambulance is dispatched.
